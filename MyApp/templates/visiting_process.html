Visiting Process

1. Arrival

Visitors will be searched. Produce the following authorized identification:

  • South African ID
  • Passport

2. Registration

  • Personal details of visitor(s)
  • Case number or registration number of the offender(s) to be visited
  • No unauthorized items (firearms, knives, etc.)
  • Leave valuables at the main gate

3. Display the visitor tag visibly at all times.

4. Body Search

Visitors will be searched before being accompanied to the relevant visiting area.

5. Waiting Area

  • Visitors are searched once more.
  • Visitors’ and offender’s/remand detainee’s personal details are verified.

6. Briefing

Visitors are briefed about the rules of the visiting area. The offender/remand detainee may decide whether or not to see the visitor.

7. Visit

  • The visitor waits for the offender/remand detainee to be located.
  • The visitor may buy delicacies from the tuck shop for consumption in the visiting area.
  • Misbehavior, such as unbecoming conducts and disturbing others, will terminate the visit immediately.
  • All visits are subject to monitoring and must take place within sight and earshot of an official.

8. End of Visit

  • The offender/remand detainee will be called first, while the visitors remain seated.
  • Visitors and offenders/remand detainees will be notified five (5) minutes before visiting time is over.
  • No offender is allowed to bring anything into the correctional center after the visit.

9. Waiting Area

  • Visitors go back to the waiting room where they will be searched before being accompanied back to the main gate.

10. Main Gates

  • Visitors must return the visitor tag.
  • Visitors who left their valuables at the gate must claim them back.
